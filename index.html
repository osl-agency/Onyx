Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Onyx

How is Onyx different from using Tor?

Unlike Tor, which routes traffic through the internet, Onyx creates a completely isolated local onion network. This eliminates all vulnerabilities associated with internet exposure while maintaining the same multi-layered encryption benefits.

Can Onyx connect to the internet?

No, and that's by design. Onyx is completely air-gapped from the internet, which eliminates entire categories of cyber threats. It operates exclusively on your local network, providing secure communications without external exposure.

How do I set up and use Onyx?

Onyx is designed for simplicity. Power it on, connect it to your local network, and access the administration panel through the secure interface. The guided setup takes less than 5 minutes, with no internet connection required at any point.

What happens if someone physically steals my Onyx device?

Onyx includes multiple physical security measures. The device detects tampering attempts and automatically wipes sensitive data. Additionally, all stored data is encrypted, requiring your unique authentication keys to access.
